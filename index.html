About Me

Hello, I’m a new web developer transitioning out of healthcare. My experience as doctor of physical therapy has garnered in me a passion for solving complex problems and doing good for others. Although I enjoyed my career, I found myself dismayed and dissatisfied with the constraints the medical system puts on creativity, innovation, and efficiency. This realization forced me to look outside of healthcare and into a field that garners innovation and isn’t burdened with numerous regulatory bodies dictating how I perform my job. The tech industry is unique in that it is built upon innovation and never being satisfied with status quo.

As a web developer, I am passionate about advancing software that will increase efficiency and improve people's quality of life. I hope to utilize my unique industry knowledge to lessen the barriers for providing quality patient care and help providers be more efficient. With the constantly increasing documentation demands on healthcare providers, efficient software is more important than ever. However, healthcare is not my only passion. I am open to opportunities in any industry that will allow me to experience continual learning and apply my skills to improve people’s lives.
